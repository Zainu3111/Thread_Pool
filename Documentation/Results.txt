Machine:
	Lenovo i5 8th gen t580 with 16gb RAM and 1tb storage.
	Machine is multicore and hyperthreaded with 8 total threads.

Testing isPrime benchmark for Sequential solution ran in 512ms.

Testing isPrime benchmark for Naive Multi-Threaded solution ran in 149ms.
A speed up of 3.4 times

Testing isPrime with a basic Threadpool for Multi-Threaded solution runs in 96 ms.
A speed up of 5.34 times over sequential solution and 1.5 times over the Naive Threaded approach.

Testing isPrime with a waiting threadpool for multi threaded gives 101 ms.
Approxiamtely the same as the basic threadpool. This is possibly because of the fact that the work queue always have tasks in it. However, it should be much more efficient on the cpu.
